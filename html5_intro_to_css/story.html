information about blackdesert online-mainstory

The story of black desert revolves around these thing that come from an asteroid called black spirits and they have been in a sense of war with the world. not through traditional means but more through corruption within the world of the game everything seemingly revolves around this material called black stones that where found and excavated in the great desert thes stones are valuable and give power but will also corrupt. as a player you are trying to run around retrieve your memories as well as to help those that are plagued with the issues in the world.
