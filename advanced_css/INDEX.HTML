What is baldurs gate 3?

Baldurs gate three is a turn based rpg with multiplayer elements that has currently taken the internet by storm due to its immersive story telling freedom of choices sound design combat and similarity to the table top rpg known as DND. it is a game i personally have spent hundreads of hours in somehow and still have not personally seen everything as a partially open world split into ACTS it is a game that enjoyers of DND as well as divinity and the other baldurs gate games can come to enjoy and possibily even love.
