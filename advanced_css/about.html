about Baldurs Gate three

baldurs gate 3 is a game made by larian studios with licensingfrom wizards of the coast the creaters of DND and magic the gathering it is a turn based RPG with heavy elements on the story telling and freedom of choice boasting a current critic score of 97/100 on pc gamer and a user score of 8.9 per meta critic. currently regarded as an easy candidate for game of the year it is also widely regarded as the closest a video game has gotten to the feel of the renown table top RPG.
