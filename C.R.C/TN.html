Dunlap TN

In 2002 CRC expanded operation to create a second contact center in TN this success led to the increase in both employees and customers. Around this time the CRClink was upgraded to a new generation of software in order to handle the increase of demand. Through this demand though there was an issue of space and by 2007 the dunlap center was constructed to ensure that there was adequate space to fulfill operational requirements.
